short_description: A terminal client for Miniflux RSS reader
description: >-
  cliflux is a terminal-based UI client for the self-hosted Miniflux RSS reader,
  designed to manage feeds, track read/unread status, and organize saved
  entries.  

  It offers a keyboard-driven interface with vim-like navigation (j/k), allowing
  you to quickly refresh feeds, toggle article states, and open links in your
  browser.  

  Built in Rust, the tool is highly efficient and provides a lightweight way to
  consume your RSS feeds without leaving the terminal.
