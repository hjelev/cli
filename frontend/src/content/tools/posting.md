  Textual TUI framework.  

  It delivers a keyboard-centric, Vim-friendly experience akin to Postman or
  Insomnia directly inside your terminal.  

  Requests are stored locally as clean, version-controllable YAML files, and the
  app supports environment variables, syntax highlighting, and curl imports.
